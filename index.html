About Me

Personal Interests

Outside of my professional life, I am passionate about traveling, photography, and exploring new cultures. I believe that experiencing different parts of the world broadens one's perspective and enhances creativity. I also enjoy reading, particularly books on personal development, technology, and history. These hobbies not only allow me to relax but also help me grow as a person, providing a well-rounded balance to my technical career.

Education background

Apart from freeCodeCamp online courses and a year in Vocational School for Software Development in Germany, I worked as a Software Developer where I tackled new challenges daily and had the chance to continuously develop my programming skills with interesting projects. This blend of technical and soft skills positions me well for tackling complex challenges and contributing effectively to any team.

Future Goals

Looking ahead, I am committed to further enhancing my technical skills and deepening my expertise in fields such as artificial intelligence, software development, and best practices in development. I also aspire to collaborate with like-minded professionals across the globe. My long-term goal is to work on innovative projects that address some of the world's most pressing challenges, to make a meaningful impact on society.

Tech & Talents

Balancing roles as a waiter and receptionist, I've sharpened my communication skills and embraced self-learning. These experiences have taught me to handle multiple responsibilities efficiently, adapt to various work environments, and maintain a customer-centric approach. My coding journey involves mastering HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and expanding my Python expertise using the Django framework. I also have some experience in React, jQuery MongoDB, sql and git. In addition to my technical skills, I have cultivated a problem-solving mindset and a passion for continuous learning.

Language Journey

Throughout my education, I dedicated myself to improving my English fluency, which blossomed through daily interactions with guests during my time as a receptionist and a waiter. While residing in Germany, I was actively enhancing my German language skills, embracing daily communication as a pathway to continuous self-improvement. I am also learning Portugese in my spare time. This linguistic journey has not only expanded my communication abilities but also deepened my understanding of different cultures and perspectives.
